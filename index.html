Tokyo Drift - The Game

~ Big Mouth Billy ~

In an effort to get a fleeting moment of peace, our hero Billy has snuck out of the Rapid7 office for a sushi lunch.

Unfortunately, right as his sushi arrived, the drifters set out on a mission to find him to help solve their endless problems.

Do you part and help Billy eat as much sushi as possible before he is caught by three drifters and has to go back to work!

If you help him eat enough, he may even let you in on a little secret...
